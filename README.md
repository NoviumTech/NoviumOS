## What is NoviumOS?

NoviumOS is a Linux-based operating system built for people who are tired of bloat. Windows eats your RAM. macOS locks you into expensive hardware. Other Linux distros feel lifeless.

NoviumOS is **clean. Smooth. Yours.**

Built on Arch Linux with a focus on animations, responsiveness, and respecting your hardware.

## Why NoviumOS?

- **No bloatware** — Only what you need, nothing more
- **Smooth animations** — Designed to feel premium and responsive
- **Minimal by default** — Your computer respects your time
- **Open source** — Built in the open, for everyone
- **Actually usable** — We dogfood it daily
